I’m one of the several people in the US who can never give you a straight answer when you ask where they’re from – I was born in Hong Kong, grew up in Shanghai, but regularly visited family in the Bay Area, where I am now located for college. Both my parents speak Cantonese, yet I only ever learned to speak Mandarin, which has been getting rusty anyways. To toss some quick facts out there:

  • I spent much of high school trying to spread Ultimate Frisbee throughout Shanghai and nearly skipped my graduation ceremony to attend a tournament final
  • I grew up loving all types of cars, but never got to drive until I permanently moved to the US
  • I’ve spent half a day lining up at grand openings of major boba stores, simply because
  • I love chatting about ethical issues/implications revolving around cutting-edge technologies
